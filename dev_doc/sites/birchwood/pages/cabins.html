Minnesota Cabin Rentals on the Lake

White Birch Resort has 10 BRAND NEW high end custom built vacation home and cabins, ranging from 640 sq. ft. one room studio cabins to deluxe 3600 sq. ft. six bedroom entertainment reunion homes for up to 22 guests. All 10 deluxe size accommodations can host groups of up to 128 guests on the resort year round. Guests can choose from any of the beautifully designed rental home and lake cabin rental MN ranging from a “budget friendly” one room studio cabin to multiple expansive six bedroom private family entertainment reunion homes.

All building interior and exteriors are architecturally designed and personally accessorized with a great deal of attention to detail. Each design inspiration is beautifully decorated and furnished with a careful blend of stylish antiques, contemporary art and modern Northwood’s furnishings providing the utmost in comfort in combination with a NEW “Unique Rustic Experience”.

Guests are able to choose from many iconic lodge themes. They can choose from themes, such as, canoeing and moose in the Gunflint territory, to the lake trout found in the deep waters of Lake Superior at Duluth to logging in the Northwoods of Blackduck, Bemidji MN and surrounding areas. All northern Minnesota cabin rentals are keeping with the heart of lake country and nature in mind, perfect for friends and families alike. Guests feel as though they are at their own special private lake home. Most home plans feature expansive decks and screen porches for outdoor entertaining. House rental and/or cabin rental packages can be tailored to offer pontoon, private hot tub, golf cart(s), ski boat rentals and much more for any stay. “You tell us your interests, and we’ll make it happen”. White Birch Resort is proud to be known as the ONLY Minnesota vacation resort facility in the state who accommodates luxury cabin rentals in Minnesota with their own 12-person private in ground stone commercial grade hot tubs! This is truly unique in the vacation rentals resort industry!!

These luxury vacation homes and cabin rentals are located just minutes north of Bemidji, 3 1/2 hours north of Minneapolis st, Twin Cities, St Paul United States, 3 hours east of Fargo and 1 hour south of Canada.

Even though WBR has ample amounts of on resort entertainment, rest assured, the area also features countless access to over 90 local attractions and activities, such as golfing, hiking in Chippewa National Forest, biking in Bemidji State Park, cross country skiing and downhill skiing, snow tubing at Buena Vista Ski Resort, site seeing, shopping at your favorite gift shop and much more, if you so choose to explore off the resort.

Kids Stay FREE the months of May and June (Ask for Details). This is the best time to go fishing in Minnesota for walleye. Call for more details on what lodging rentals are available. White Birch Resort also offers fishing guide service for Blackduck Lake, Upper Red Lake and all surrounding lakes.
